# Question 1: What did you build and how does it work?

I built a customized, secure(password protected) Distributor Resource Portal that allows our partners to easily browse and access marketing assets, training guides, and documentation. The portal automatically updates on the frontend whenever a new post is added in WordPress, organizing materials chronologically into a clean, modern grid layout. Each resource card automatically pulls the asset's title, publication date, and category tag so our distributors can instantly find what they need. Finally, the file download system is designed to be completely foolproof, meaning it will safely link and deliver files to our partners no matter how the digital assets are uploaded or formatted on the backend.

# Question 2: What was the most challenging part and how did you solve it?

The most challenging part of the project was recovering from an unexpected platform failure that erased my entire initial build on InstaWP, including all posts, ACF fields, and custom logic. To keep the project on track, I migrated to a new environment on TasteWP and rebuilt the portal from scratch, recreating the structure and functionality under tight time constraints. During the rebuild, I also encountered rendering issues caused by modern block‑based WordPress themes overriding my custom PHP output. Switching to a classic theme restored full template control and allowed the category‑based WP_Query loop to function correctly. These adjustments ensured the final distributor portal remained stable, clean, and fully aligned with the project requirements.
